What You can Do?

You can make a difference to people who need your help! Your pledge of $50 or more for the ICC helps support the local community through services like our free medical clinic as well as our subsidized senior and youth programs.
Join hundreds of walkers, runners, adults and kids will gather at beautiful Baylands Park in Sunnyvale to participate in Sevathon 2014, celebrating the idea of service and giving back to the community.
